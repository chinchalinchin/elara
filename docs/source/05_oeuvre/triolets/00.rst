I: Forever
----------

| Let this moment last forever
| Let it sink not into never
| For all our plans so clever
| Let this moment last forever 
| Hidden goal in each endeavor
| Lifted load of every lever
| Let this moment last forever
| Let it sink not into never.

- February 2025
