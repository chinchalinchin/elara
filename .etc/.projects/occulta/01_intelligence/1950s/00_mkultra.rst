**MEMORANDUM FOR: THE RECORD**

January 1956

1. The purpose of Subproject 54 is to continue work on the mechanism of brain concussion. Mr. :redacted:`REDACTED` has recently completd a prelimnary study under a :redacted:`REDACTED` contract.
2. The scope of the research program will be to pursue studies on the resonance-cavitation theory of brain concussion using a fluid filled glass simulated skull. In addition to the above, extensive cadaver impoact experiments will be carried out for possible correlation with the above theory. For a more detailed explanation of the scope of the program see the attached proposal.
3. The cost of this program is to be $60,000 for a period of one year starting 1 Februrary 1956. To this sum must be added $2,4000 representing a four percent service charge to the :redacted:`REDACTED`. Therefore, the total cost will not exceed $62,400. Charges should be made against Allotmeent 6-2502-10-001.
4. The :redacted:`REDACTED` has requested the :redacted:`REDACTED` to submit to it a summary account of monies received from the :redacted:`REDACTED`.
5. Upon terminiation of the project or withdrawl of financial support by the sponsor, disposal of permanent equipment shall follow the schedule (Para. a-e) under "Proposed Budget", set forth in the attached proposla.
6. It was mutually agreed that documentation and accounting for travel expenses which are reimbursable by the :redacted:`REDACTED` shall conform with the accepted practices of that corporation.
7. Mr. :redacted:`REDACTED` agreed to comply with the requirements of the Memorandum of Agreement.

- :handwritten:`Sidney Gottlieb` , Chief, TSS/Chemical Division

31 August 1956 

1. The purpose of this subproject is to continue to make available the services of Mr. :redacted:`REDACTED`. These services concern the application of the magician's techniques to clandestine operations. Such techniques include the surreptitious delivery of materials, deceptive moments and actions to cover normally prohibited activities. In addition to these services, Mr. :redacted:`REDACTED` will continue to be a consultant for the development of various forms of disguise and covery signalling systems.
2. The cost of this program is estimated to be $1500.00 for a period of one year. Charges should be made against Allotment 7-2502-10-001.
3. Mr. :redacted:`REDACTED` will submit invoices for reimbursement of his services. It is expected that his services will not exceed $200.00 per week; however, it is possible that unusual circumstance might arise wherein this sum could be exceeded.
   
- :handwritten:`Sidney Gottlieb`, Chief, TSS/Chemical Division
