--------
Jasilynn
--------

Sonnet #1
^^^^^^^^^

.. verse::
    :increment: 5

    this heart was made to pump my blood around,
    to feel the sun on fingertips inside
    the chambers where the currents run aground
    and darkness leaves the warmth of it implied.

    commotion sweeps away what parts collide,
    as skin begins to stick to her again
    and thoughts recede upon the breaking tide,
    the gentle lips of sighing Jasilynn.

    the ocean claims the lives of countless men,
    their water graves become the pulse of veins;
    the drying words of ink within my pen
    are made of what their ancient blood contains.

    her eyes awash in mine, we drift and sway
    to let the waves convey our hearts away.

- June 2026

Sonnet #2
^^^^^^^^^

.. verse::
    :increment: 5

    my ears are deaf to music in your mind
    though eyes betray the notes it softly plays,
    in ways your stare invades with aims designed
    to keep my own with yours in steady gaze.

    mistakes are made because my fingers graze
    the pipes that gently shape your tongue to speak,
    and feel the sounds before they breach airways,
    the tremors building up from cheek to cheek.
    
    what words escape are afterthoughts that leak
    despite restraint you place upon their choice;
    what tears then follow after only seek
    the force contained within your inner voice.

    the things unsaid then linger there awhile
    as lips that tremble try their best to smile.

- July 2026
