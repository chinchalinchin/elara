--------
Jasilynn
--------

Sonnet #1
---------

.. verse::
    :increment: 5

    this heart was made to pump my blood around,
    to feel the sun on fingertips inside
    the chambers where the currents run aground
    and darkness leaves the warmth of it implied.

    commotion sweeps away what parts collide,
    as skin begins to stick to her again
    and thoughts recede upon the breaking tide,
    the gentle lips of sighing Jasilynn.

    the ocean claims the lives of countless men,
    their water graves become the pulse of veins;
    the drying words of ink within my pen
    are made of what their ancient blood contains.

    her eyes awash in mine, we drift and sway
    to let the waves convey our hearts away.

- June 2026
