Πόλεμος
-------

.. epigraph::

    Before man was, war waited from him.

    -- `Judge Holden <https://www.forbes.com/sites/zacharyfolk/2026/03/08/black-toxic-rain-falls-on-tehran-after-air-strikes-hit-oil-refineries/>`_

| arrested pink of morning star's descent
| ablaze again above the Eden peaks
| 
| while cradled drops like fallen angels rent
| the infant sky with showered sparking streaks
|
| phosphoric rains that drill beneath the ground
| infernal course from heaven bent to hell
|
| bewailed below in crowds of motion drowned
| through writhing streets where ancient ruins dwell
|
| metallic veins engorged with blackened blood
| encircled serpentine the cold machine
|
| each petal bloom of flame in wicked bud
| unfolded plumes through fumes of gasoline.
|
| at dawn, the cities burned like oiled lamps
| til evening stars retreated to their camps

- March 2026
